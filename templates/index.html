Who are we ?

The Association for Research and Knowledge in Astronomy or ARKA, is the official Astronomy and Physics club of IIT(ISM) Dhanbad

About Us.

ARKA stands for the Association for Research and Knowledge in Astronomy, and it is the official Astronomy and Physics club of IIT(ISM) Dhanbad. The club was established on 5th December 2017 under the able leadership of six esteemed seniors of the batch of 2017.
We are a team of die-hard space enthusiasts who admires the excellence of astronomy and space science and seek to spread its magnificence around us.

Our Activities

Arka Events

To help its members gain technical expertise in astronomy, ARKA holds frequent competitions, seminars, space talks, and events with prominent scientists and researchers. The club also has its very own state-of-the-art Dobsonian telescope to explore the marvels of the night sky.
